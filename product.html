Preparations Instructions

Simply add 5 table spoons of “the flour” into a bowl and pour a little water (approximately 50ml) whole stirring to make a thick smooth paste. Then pour additional cold or hot water while stirring for your desired thickness. for a creamier taste add cold or hot milk instead of water.
